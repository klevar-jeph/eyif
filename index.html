About Event

Empowered Youth,
Transformed Future

Youth constitute the backbone of our state's development. As the drivers of creativity, energy, and innovation, young people must be inspired and equipped with practical skills, entrepreneurial thinking, and civic awareness. This event seeks to rally national attention on youth empowerment, showcasing innovation, entrepreneurship, and civic participation as vital tools for shaping a prosperous and inclusive future.
